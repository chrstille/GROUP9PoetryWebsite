-- ABOUT --
Users can publish poems they can think in this project if they have no plans on publishing a book, this project is a space open for creativity and fun. 
They can also view poems that were already added/seeded to give them ideas what poems to write or make!

-- ACCOUNT CREATION -- 
Users can create account with username, email, and password. The password is then hashed so that their password don't appear as plaintext in the database.

-- ACCOUNT LOGIN --
Users can login using their username and password they provided during account creation.

-- MAIN FEED -- 
As displayed on the main feed, the poems are displayed with a number (their ID) on the top-right corner. The poems are displayed from latest to oldest addition.
From the main feed, users can see the title, author, single line of the poem, and the anthology of the poem. 

-- READ FULL POEM --
Since there are cases where poems can be quite long, users can view the full poem by pressing the "Read Full" link that can be found on the bottom-right of the poem cards. Clicking the link opens a modal that shows the title, author, full poem, and the anthology of the poem (bottom-right corner of the modal) is classified under. If there is no anthology, then there will be no anthology to display.

-- LIKING POEMS --
Users can like poems that they are in favor of by pressing the heart icon at the bottom of the poem card. It then shows a filled up heart icon, and this saves a record in the database. When users log out and log in back in their account, the poems they liked before will stay liked, which displays the heart icon still filled up.

-- POEM CREATION --
If the user wants to write a poem, they can press on the button found at the bottom-right corner of their screen. 
They are given the freedom to think of a title for their poem, the creative verse/poem that they want to share, and lastly the option to select existing anthologies or create a new anthology.

-- SORT BY ANTHOLOGY (SIDEBAR) --
If the user wants to view poems that belong to a specific anthology, they can sort their main feed by selecting anthologies from the sidebar (which can be found in the right side of their screen).
There's also an option to view poems that aren't classified under an anthology
